Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Herman Deol, DDS

Born and raised in Southern Ontario, Canada, Dr. Deol brings academic excellence and genuine compassion to every patient relationship.

After earning his Honors Bachelor of Science from McMaster University, he received his Doctor of Dental Surgery from the University of Detroit Mercy — graduating at the top of his class. But what patients remember most isn't his credentials. It's how he makes them feel.
